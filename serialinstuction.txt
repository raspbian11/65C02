TO OPEN SERIAL

WINDOWS key + r
type powershell
press enter
copy and paste following command
ss -com:14 -baud:19200 -databits:8 -stopbits:1 -parity:none -forcenewline:on
-- this make SImplySerial use
1 stop bit
8 bit
19,200 BAUD
NO PARADY
New line per transmission
